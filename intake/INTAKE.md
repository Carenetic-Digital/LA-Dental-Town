# Project Intake — LA Dental Town

## Business Information

**Business Name:** LA Dental Town
**Tagline:** Healthy Smiles. Happy Lives. | Quality Care for the Whole Family
**Industry:** Healthcare — Dental Practice (General, Cosmetic & Specialty)
**Location:** East Los Angeles, CA
**Current Site:** https://www.ladentaltown.com
**Client Contact:** Norma Adams
**Agency Team:** Nathan Norberg, Carrie (Skyhook Interactive)

## Target Audience

**Primary audience:** Families in East Los Angeles and surrounding communities seeking general, cosmetic, and specialty dental care. Spanish-speaking patients are a key segment (Se Habla Español is a featured differentiator).
**Secondary audience:** Patients seeking specific specialty care — pediatric dentistry, dental implants, oral surgery, endodontics, cosmetic/Invisalign.
**What action should visitors take?** Book an appointment via the practice management booking widget. Secondary: call the office, explore services, learn about the doctors.

## Reference Sites

**Primary reference:** Bel Air Dental Arts — overall look, feel, layout, sticky Book Now CTA, floating Google Reviews badge, testimonials style
**Additional inspiration:**
- Trusted Dental Group (Hawthorne) — hero video treatment above the fold

## Brand Voice

**Tone:** Warm, professional, and trustworthy. Family-oriented and approachable, while emphasizing clinical excellence and advanced technology. Not cold or clinical.
**Key messages:**
- East Los Angeles community dental practice — rooted here, serving families
- Top-rated doctors with elite credentials (UCSF, UC Berkeley, UOP, Boston University)
- Advanced technology for a comfortable, modern experience
- Full-spectrum care under one roof — general, cosmetic, pediatric, implants, oral surgery, endodontics
- Walk-ins welcome; Se Habla Español; most PPO & Denti-Cal accepted

**Practice differentiators (feature prominently as icon strip on homepage):**
- Walk-Ins Welcome
- Se Habla Español
- PPO & Medical Accepted
- Convenient Appointments
- Advanced Technology
- Beautiful, Natural Results
