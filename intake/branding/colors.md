# Brand Colors — LA Dental Town

Extracted from the client's marketing flyer and Meet the Doctors PDF. Use as the definitive color reference.

> **Note from client:** Gold is present in marketing materials as an accent only — Norma confirmed she doesn't love gold as a dominant color. Use sparingly as a decorative accent (sparkles, dividers, icons). Let the greens lead. Cream background gives warmth without feeling clinical.

## Primary Colors

**Primary Dark Green:** `#1D4D2F`
- Used for: Logo, footer bar, nav elements, badge backgrounds, primary headings on light backgrounds

**Medium Green:** `#3A7D44`
- Used for: CTA buttons, price text, brush stroke accents, hover states, links

**Gold / Warm Yellow:** `#C9A84C`
- Used for: Sparkle accents, heart icons, decorative dividers, star ratings — accent only, not dominant

**Dark Teal:** `#163232`
- Used for: Secondary dark panel backgrounds (e.g., "Scan to Book" section, dark footer sections)

## Neutral / Background Colors

**Cream / Off-White:** `#F8F4EC`
- Used for: Page background, section backgrounds — primary background color, gives warmth

**White:** `#FFFFFF`
- Used for: Card backgrounds, text on dark backgrounds, form fields

**Near-Black:** `#1A1A1A`
- Used for: Body text, headings on light backgrounds

## Semantic Colors

**Success:** #10b981 (Emerald)
**Warning:** #f59e0b (Amber)
**Error:** #ef4444 (Red)

## Usage Guidance

- Backgrounds: Alternate between Cream (`#F8F4EC`) and White (`#FFFFFF`) sections for visual rhythm; use Dark Green (`#1D4D2F`) or Dark Teal (`#163232`) for high-contrast dark sections (footer, CTA panels)
- CTAs: Medium Green (`#3A7D44`) for primary buttons; Dark Green (`#1D4D2F`) for hover
- Accents: Gold (`#C9A84C`) sparingly — icons, dividers, star ratings only
- Text: Near-Black (`#1A1A1A`) on light backgrounds; White (`#FFFFFF`) on dark backgrounds

## Color Format Notes

For Tailwind v4 with oklch, colors will be converted automatically.
